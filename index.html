About Rajasthan Virtual Shiksha

Rajasthan Virtual Shiksha is a Smart India Hackathon 2025 project designed to bridge the educational gap between urban and rural areas. Our platform provides quality education to diploma college students in rural Rajasthan, overcoming challenges of poor internet connectivity and limited resources.

The platform is optimized for low-bandwidth environments, allowing students to access educational content even with 2G/3G connections. With features like offline access, downloadable content, and multi-language support, we aim to make quality education accessible to all.
